About Us

Empowering youth and families with innovative mental health solutions.

Who We Are

Tomorrow's Hope was founded in June 2023 myself (Kit). I myself have struggled a lot with my own mental health. Struggling with depression from 12 years old, and having been in therapy since I was 8 - I found that I struggle to communicate my thoughts and feelings to my parents.

After doing some more research, and talking to a few friends and mental health professionals I found that it's really common to have difficulty communicating with your parent. Or indeed vice versa, a parent talking to their young person. That's where Tomorrow's Hope comes in.

The Co-founder of Tomorrow's Hope (and my good friend) Michael, came on board in December 2023, after we noticed similarities in experieces, passions, and drive to make this tool accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible and innovative mental health resources that empower individuals to take charge of their well-being. We aim to build a supportive community where everyone feels heard and valued.

Our Vision

We envision a world where mental health is prioritised, talked about without stigma. A world where not only every young person has access to the tools they need to thrive; but also parent/guardians and carers have the resources they need. Through collaboration, education, and technology, we aim to create a lasting impact on youth mental health care.
